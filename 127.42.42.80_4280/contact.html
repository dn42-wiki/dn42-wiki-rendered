Contact

Members of the dn42 network mostly communicate via IRC or the mailing list.

If you would like to contact an individual network operator, you will find contact information in the Whois registry.

Mailing list

  • is at: dn42 /at/ groups.io,
  • send a mail to dn42+subscribe /at/ groups.io to subscribe
  • has archive
  • can be accessed via NNTP as well. The group is alt.net.dn42.users

IRC

Channel #dn42 in hackint

See IRC too.

Telegram

  • english only, maintained by SUNNET-MNT
  • bilingual, Chinese&English, maintained by BILL-MNT
  • spanish, maintained by BUZZ-MNT

Web

There are currently multiple decentralized mirrors and domains for the DN42 wiki available:

  • dn42.us maintained by xuu
  • dn42.dev / wiki.burble.dn42 maintained by Burble
  • dn42.eu maintained by Nurtic-vibe
  • dn42.pp.ua maintained by Androw
  • dn42.wiki maintained by DannyAAM
  • dn42.de (ipv6 only)
  • dn42.cc maintained by LARE (uses wiki-ng)
